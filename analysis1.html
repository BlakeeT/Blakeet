Bruce Highway Crash Heatmap

The most dangerous road in Australia.


I was interested in crash data within Queensland as I was curious which road was the most dangerous. I was unable to find government resources to suit, therefore i made my own using government provided datasets.

First, I took the dataset to R for cleaning. During cleaning I removed property damage as the government discontinued tracking it throughout the dataset and found the most dangerous road in Queensland by:

Once the most dangerous road was found, I rechecked and found one invalid coordinate which was removed, renamed and transformed the dataset with only the values from the Bruce highway then did some exploratory analysis into why the road is so dangerous before exporting to Tableau for heatmap visualisation.

Once in Tableau, I wanted to create something anyone could use. This is why i chose buttons with dropdown to handle filtering; reducing clutter. Colours blue and orange are also chosen to prevent colourblindness from inhibiting the view.
